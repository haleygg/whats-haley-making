Ever wanted to design your own garment? Not sure where to start? Well, I have provided a gauge swatch calculator! First, create your gauge swatch with the yarn and needle size of your choice, and then enter your dimensions below of both your gauge swatch and then the size of the panel you wish to create. When entering the dimensions for the gauge swatch, include the width and height in inches and the number of stitches in that amount of inches.
